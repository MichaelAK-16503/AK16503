About Tokyo

Tokyo is a mesmerizing mix of cutting-edge innovation and traditional culture. From neon-lit skyscrapers to tranquil temples, this bustling city offers a travel experience like no other. Explore its districts, cuisine, and heritage.

Top Sights in Tokyo

  • Tokyo Tower – Iconic red and white tower with amazing views.
  • Shibuya Crossing – The world's busiest pedestrian crossing.
  • Asakusa & Sensō-ji Temple – Historic temple and traditional shopping street.
  • Tokyo Skytree – One of the tallest towers in the world.
  • Meiji Shrine – Peaceful forested shrine in the heart of the city.

Things to Do

  • Try sushi at Tsukiji Outer Market
  • Experience an anime café in Akihabara
  • Shop in Harajuku’s Takeshita Street
  • Relax in an onsen (hot spring bath)
  • Watch sumo training in Ryōgoku

Travel Tips

  • Get a prepaid IC card (Suica/PASMO) for easy transit access
  • Learn basic phrases like "Arigatou" (thank you) and "Sumimasen" (excuse me)
  • Be punctual—timeliness is highly valued
  • Respect quiet zones on public transport
  • Cash is still widely used, especially in local shops

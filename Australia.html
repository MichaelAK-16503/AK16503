Top Places to Visit in Australia

  • Sydney – Opera House & Harbour Bridge
  • Great Barrier Reef – Snorkeling paradise
  • Melbourne – Art, cafes, and culture
  • Uluru (Ayers Rock) – Sacred red rock in the Outback
  • Gold Coast – Beaches & theme parks

Australian Cuisine Highlights

  • Meat Pies & Sausage Rolls
  • Vegemite on Toast
  • Lamb Chops & BBQ
  • Tim Tams & Pavlova
  • Fresh seafood – Barramundi, Moreton Bay Bugs

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: September to November or March to May
  • Australia is huge – plan based on regions
  • Tap water is safe to drink
  • Sun protection is essential – bring sunscreen
  • Drive on the left side of the road

Top Destinations

  • Kuala Lumpur – The bustling capital with Petronas Towers
  • Penang – A food lover's paradise
  • Langkawi – Island escape with beaches and nature
  • Borneo – Adventure and wildlife in Sabah & Sarawak
  • Malacca – Historical charm and cultural fusion

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: December to April
  • Use Grab for convenient transportation
  • Respect local customs and dress modestly in religious sites
  • Tap water is not usually drinkable – drink bottled water

Must-Try Malaysian Dishes

  • Nasi Lemak
  • Char Kway Teow
  • Satay
  • Roti Canai
  • Laksa

Welcome to the Big Apple

New York City is a bustling metropolis filled with iconic landmarks, world-class museums, diverse neighborhoods, and unforgettable experiences. Let this guide help you make the most of your trip to NYC!

Top Attractions

  • Statue of Liberty & Ellis Island
  • Times Square
  • Central Park
  • Empire State Building
  • Broadway Theaters

Things to Do

  • Walk the Brooklyn Bridge at sunset
  • Explore art at The Met and MoMA
  • Eat your way through Chinatown or Little Italy
  • Catch a Yankees or Knicks game
  • Take a ferry to Staten Island

Travel Tips

  • Use the subway—it’s the fastest way to get around
  • Wear comfortable shoes—NYC is best explored on foot
  • Keep your belongings secure, especially in crowded areas
  • Buy attraction tickets in advance to avoid lines
  • Tipping is expected: 15-20% in restaurants
